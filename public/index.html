About the batch

Small-batch fermentation means natural variation, tighter control, and a product that still feels made by actual people.

No shortcuts. No fake wellness theater.

Komboostcha is a home fermentation project focused on good handling, clean ingredients, and batches small enough to watch properly. Kombucha is brewed, fermented, bottled, and chilled with attention. The yoghurt is cultured and strained until it earns the texture.

Where do you deliver?

General QC and Ortigas for now. If you’re just outside the edge, ask first on WhatsApp.

What does kombucha taste like?

Lightly tart, a little fizzy, and cleaner than most people expect.

How long does it stay fresh?

Kombucha keeps 2–3 weeks refrigerated. Greek yoghurt is best within 5–7 days.

Need a bulk order?

For 6+ bottles, message ahead before the batch closes so stock can be reserved properly.
