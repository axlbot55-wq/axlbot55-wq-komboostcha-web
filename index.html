What is kombucha?
Fermented tea made with a live culture called a SCOBY. It’s naturally tart, lightly fizzy, and alive in the best way.
What does it taste like?
Think crisp iced tea with a gentle tang rather than heavy sweetness. Each batch has a little personality — that’s the point.
How should I store it?
Keep everything refrigerated once received. Kombucha keeps 2–3 weeks chilled; yoghurt is best within 5–7 days.
Is this beginner-friendly?
Yes. The kombucha is brewed to stay approachable, and the yoghurt is straight-up comforting.
